## Outline

### Intro (150 words)
- "You just bought your first pressure washer and a portable water tank. You don't need $200/mo in software yet."
- Promise: a real $0 starter stack that gets you from first booking to first paid invoice, and tells you exactly when to upgrade.

### The starter stack (categorized)

For each category, name the best free tool, what its ceiling is, and when to upgrade.

1. **Booking & scheduling** — Google Calendar + a simple booking link (Calendly free tier, or Cal.com self-hosted)
2. **Client list / CRM** — Google Contacts + Sheets, until you're past ~50 clients
3. **Invoicing** — Wave (genuinely free, unlimited invoices) or Square Invoices (free, transaction fee only)
4. **Payments** — Square or Stripe (no monthly fee, per-transaction only)
5. **Before / after photos** — phone camera + Google Photos (free tier)
6. **Website / booking page** — Cloudflare Pages or Google Sites or a Squarespace trial
7. **Business email** — Gmail on your own name for the first month; move to a domain when you buy one
8. **Bookkeeping** — Wave Accounting (free) or a Google Sheet with the template we link

### When to upgrade each piece
Simple table: "If X is true → move from free tool to paid tool"
- "You have >30 recurring clients and are double-booking" → move to Urable / Mobile Tech RX
- "Wave stops matching your income to your bookings" → move to QuickBooks Self-Employed
